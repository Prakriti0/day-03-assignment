Shrinkhala Khatiwada-Miss Nepal World 2018

Shrinkhala Khatiwada (Nepali: शृङ्खला खतिवडा, pronounced [ˈsriŋkʰʌla ˈkʰʌt̪iwʌɽa]) is a Nepalese model and Miss Nepal World 2018.[1][2][3] She represented Nepal in Miss World 2018. She also became the second Nepali after Ishani Shrestha to win Beauty with a Purpose Award.[4] She was in the Top 12 of Miss World 2018.Khatiwada was born on 3 November 1995 (17 Kartik 2052 BS) in Hetauda, Bagmati Province. Her father, Birodh Khatiwada, is a CPN (UML) leader who was elected as a member of central parliament from Makwanpur Constituency No. 2 in general elections of 2017 for the fourth time. Her mother, Munu Sigdel Khatiwada, is the President of CPN (UML), Makwanpur District and also a member of the state parliament of Bagmati Province. She has an older brother Bimba, who is currently residing in Australia. Shrinkhala is a National Chancellor for the International Association of Educators for World Peace (IAEWP) and President of the One Home Foundation.

Shrinkhala Khatiwada, Miss Nepal 2018 exited from Top 12, as Miss Thailand advanced to the Final 5. Miss Thailand came ahead of Miss Nepal and Miss New Zealand winning Miss Asia and Miss Oceania and secured a place in Top 5. Shrinkhala Khatiwada will return to her home country with Multimedia Award and Beauty With a Purpose Award. Miss Mexico was crowned Miss World 2018. Shrinkhala Khatiwada secured a place in the final round, Top 30 of Miss World 2018 after winning Multimedia Award and Beauty With a Purpose Award. In the first round of Head to Head challenge, she won against Miss Luxembourg, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Guam, and Moldova. However, she was defeated by Miss Singapore in the 2nd round of Head to Head Challenge. She also secured runner-up position in the Sanya Tourism Promotional Video Award. Earlier, her wardrobe malfunction during her walk in the Dress Round became the hot gossip of the beauty pageant.
